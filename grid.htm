Look At The World For YourSelf

The Mongolia

Mongolia experiences an extreme continental climate characterized by long, cold winters and short summers. The capital, Ulaanbaatar, is notably cold. The country is often referred to as the "Land of the Blue Sky" due to its numerous clear days annually.

The England

England has a long and complex history, from the Roman conquest and Anglo-Saxon settlement to the Norman Conquest in 1066. It was the birthplace of the Industrial Revolution and a central part of the British Empire.

The France

French culture is deeply rooted in its history, from the time of the Gauls and Roman annexation to the French Revolution of 1789, which produced the Declaration of the Rights of Man.

The Japan

Japan is a global leader in pop culture, art, and cuisine, attracting millions of visitors annually.

The Italy

Italian culture is deeply influential, as the birthplace of the Roman civilization, the Catholic Church (home to Vatican City), and the Renaissance. This legacy is evident in its vast number of UNESCO World Heritage Sites (over 60), the most of any country.
